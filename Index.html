Emergency Go Bag Essentials

Emergency Water & Food

Bring at least one liter of clean water for hydration during emergencies. Pack non-perishable food items such as energy bars and canned goods.

First Aid Kit

Include all needed supplies like bandages, antiseptics, and pain relievers to handle minor injuries during emergencies.

Flashlight & Whistle

Use a reliable flashlight to see clearly during emergency situations. Use the whistle to signal for help and make rescuers aware of your location.

Extra Clothes

Bring a change of clothes to stay warm and dry during an evacuation. Include weather-appropriate items for your region.
